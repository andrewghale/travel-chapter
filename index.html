Welcome

The Travel Chapter is proud to have built a reputation as one of the UK’s leading holiday letting agencies with a quality collection of holiday homes across the UK.

We believe that letting your holiday property should be simple, stress-free and most of all, a success which delivers you a healthy return.

Our network of unique brands allows us to reach millions of customers not available anywhere else. From our flagship brand, holidaycottages.co.uk to our lifestyle and regional websites across the country, when you let with the Travel Chapter your property will benefit from the widest possible exposure.

Whether you’re looking to let to your holiday home for the first time, seeking some advice before buying, building or converting or you’re a seasoned and experienced holiday home owner considering other options, our team of industry experts are here to offer you a personalised service, so you can enjoy peace of mind knowing your property is in safe hands.
